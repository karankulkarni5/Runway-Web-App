About

What is Runway?

Runway is the stage that gives opportunity for an entrepreneurial idea to turn into a reality. Runway hosts the battle between aspiring entrepreneurs over 3 stages with a cash prize to kickstart their ideas.

Our Vision

We at Viral Fission devote our energy in making sure we are adding value to India’s Gen Z community and help them hone their skill sets. As the number of Gen-Zers starting their business is progressively increasing, we want to cater them an opportunity where they build a strong foundation and earn exciting cash prizes to fuel up their ideas to take a flight.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Team Size: Min: 1 and Max: 4
  • Plagiarism will not be tolerated.
  • If any plagiarism is found, the team will be disqualified at any point of time.
  • Only one member from the team has to register on the portal on behalf of the whole team.
  • A team cannot submit more than one entry.
  • A person can only be a part of one team.
  • No entries will be accepted after the deadline.
  • Students across all colleges in India are eligible to participate.
  • Any submission received after the deadline will not be accepted.
  • The participant applying should be a student with a valid ID card.
  • Selection will be based on the parameters set.
  • Go through the task description accurately, in order to avoid confusion.
  • All decisions regarding eligibility and final judgement shall rest with the Viral Fission judging panel. The VF team reserves the right to change the format or rules of the round before it starts.

Frequently Asked Questions


I want to participate in Runway? How do I apply for the competition?

To apply, click on the Register Tab and apply for Round 1 by filling in the required details and a questionnaire related to your business. Only the team leader has to register the team on the portal.

Is there any registration fee?

There is NO registration fee to be paid.

What are the timelines of the competition?

To view details about Runway please check the tab “Timelines”.

What should be the size of the team?

The team strength can be anywhere between 1 to 4. No more than 4 members can participate in the competition.

Do I compulsorily need to have a team?

A team can consist of only one member.

Can my team have members from different colleges/ cities?

Yes, there is no compulsion on having a team from a particular college. A team can consist of members from any college or city in India.

Who will be judging my application?

The first two rounds will be judged by the internal team at Viral Fission. Final participants will be judged by the panel on the spot at the finale.

Can I take part in Runway if I took part last year?

Last year participants can register again although we would suggest you apply with a different idea this season.

How will I know if I have been shortlisted for the next round?

You will receive an official communication from our team at Viral Fission on your registered email address.

If I don’t answer any question from the questionnaire. Is it acceptable?

Teams must answer all questions in the registration form. If any application is found incomplete then it won’t be considered for further evaluation.

What are the requirements of each round?

You can find all information about the requirements of each individual round by clicking on “details”.
